About

Science Carnival is a 3 day event running in DCU to promote engagement between junior cycle students and the sciences. The event is suitable for junior cycle science students - in higher or ordinary level - the only thing you need to participate is curiosity.

You can think of Science Carnival as being like circuit training for the mind. On the day we'll have stations set up with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology activities to particiapte in. After 10-15 minutes students move to the next station and the learning continues!
